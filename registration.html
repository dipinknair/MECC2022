Registration through IFAC PaperCept is mandatory for all conference and workshop participants.

Conference registrations include the registration packet, access to technical sessions, any virtual societal events at the conference, and the conference e-proceedings.

Full Member/Nonmember registrations entitle the attendee to upload up to two final manuscripts. Additional uploads (beyond two) can be purchased at 200 USD per upload. If a final submission exceeds 6 pages, you must first update the final submission information and then complete payment of the excess page charges through your conference registration account. Student members of IEEE/ASME may use the Member registration category if they are the sole registering author of an accepted paper, poster, abstract or student competition.

MECC 2022 will provide significant student travel support to participate at the conference. Please check details on the STUDENTS page.

Cancellation Policy

  • To cancel your registration, please notify the Registration Chair (Bristow, Douglas A. < dbristow@mst.edu >) in writing.
  • If your registration was used to upload a paper, poster, or abstract, then it cannot be canceled even if the paper, poster, or abstract were withdrawn.
  • Reufnds will be made if the cancellation of the conference registration occurs by July 25, 2022, with an administration cost of USD 100. No refunds can be provided for cancellations after July 25, 2022.
  • All refunds will be issued within 30 days after the conference.
